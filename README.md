# 29 Equity

**Live commercial real estate market intelligence platform**

🔗 [www.29equity.com](https://www.29equity.com)

## Overview

29 Equity aggregates live financing rates, deal activity, and benchmark analytics into a single platform for institutional CRE owners and investors. Built solo, self funded, with no co-founder and no technical hire.

## What It Does

**Predictive AI & Underwriting Analytics**
- AI rate forecasting model projecting Bear, Base, and Bull cap rate scenarios across user defined hold periods
- AI deal pricing model synthesizing comparable sales and cap rate benchmarks into acquisition price recommendations
- Acquisition and financing model calculating IRR, with a live debt sizing sensitivity slider across rate, LTV, and amortization
- AI tools extracting and ranking buyer, lender, and investor parties by transaction volume across live deal flow
- Automated article summarization surfacing deal terms, parties, and pricing from live news and data sources

**Data Pipeline & Market Coverage**
- 16 live data feeds aggregated into one platform
- Coverage across 5 CRE asset classes: Multifamily, Industrial, Office, Retail, Data Centers
- Live rate and cap rate benchmarking across 11 major markets, plus custom market search
- Automated data refresh every 6 hours
